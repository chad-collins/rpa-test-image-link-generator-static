How to Use

Copy and paste your raw image path into the field. You can even use log lines as long as it contains: 

        2022_48_7_1184612764_image_1dd8e27a-2f7b-4e6c-6345-dase0f4f28a9.png

After clicking submit, the application will provide you a usable screenshot link.
